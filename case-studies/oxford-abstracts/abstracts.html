Oxford Abstracts

Conference Submission Management System

Project: Abstracts

Live App Source Code

Problem Overview

Oxford Abstracts is an academic conference abstract and paper management system, hinging on a complex user permission system across 7 different user types and cross-over permissions. Their previous developers had overshot their delivery date by 8 months without delivering, so they came to us to build a new abstract management system that corrected the previous challenges they had experienced; the product had to be stable, scalable, maintainable and hit every deadline.

Solution Overview

The project manager worked closely with the client to understand and refine a backlog of almost 200 feature requests into an MVP scope, where the functionality is to allow for abstract submission, review and acceptance within 1 platform.
